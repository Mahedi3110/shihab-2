Criminal Law

Criminal law is the body of laws that
prohibit and punishe acts that injure
individual people, property and the entire
community.

Child Support

Child support refers to the sum that the
noncustodial parent must pay to the custodian. This sum serves as a parental contribution for the child's basic living expenses.

Tax Law

tax law, body of rules under which a public
authority has a claim on taxpayers,
requiring them to transfer to the authority
